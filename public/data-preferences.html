Data Preferences

Last updated: June 2025

Makrofy gives you full control over your personal data. Here's how you can manage it.

Your Data

Makrofy stores the following data linked to your account:

  • Email address and display name
  • Daily meal logs and nutrition data
  • Calorie and macro targets
  • Body metrics (weight, height, age, activity level)
  • Workout programs and exercise history
  • Subscription status

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not track you across apps or websites
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • We do not store your food photos permanently

Data on Your Device

Step count and motion data are read from your device's sensors and are never uploaded to our servers. They remain entirely on your device.

Meal data is cached locally for offline access and synced when you're back online.

Delete Your Data

You can delete your entire account and all associated data from Profile > Delete My Account within the app. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Alternatively, email us at omeris343212@gmail.com to request manual data deletion.
