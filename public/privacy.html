Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Makrofy ("we", "us", "the app") values your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you use our app and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we store your email address and display name via Firebase Authentication. If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive only the information you authorize.

Nutrition & meal data: The foods you log, calorie and macro targets, and meal history are stored in Firebase Firestore and linked to your account.

Photos: If you use the AI food scan feature, food photos are sent to our analysis service for nutritional estimation. Photos are processed in real time and are not stored permanently on our servers.

Motion & activity data: With your permission, we access your device's motion sensor (pedometer) to display your daily step count and estimated calories burned. This data stays on your device and is not uploaded.

Purchase information: Subscriptions and in-app purchases are processed through the Apple App Store and managed by RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment details (credit card, billing address, etc.).

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely to:

  • Provide and improve the core functionality of the app (meal tracking, nutrition analysis, fitness features)
  • Verify your subscription status
  • Sync your data across your devices

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Makrofy uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and cloud functions
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management
  • OpenAI — AI-powered food photo analysis (no personal data is sent; only food images)

Each service operates under its own privacy policy. No advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks are used.

Data Tracking

Makrofy does not track you across other apps or websites. We do not use IDFA, fingerprinting, or any cross-app tracking technology.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers (Google Cloud). All network communication uses HTTPS encryption. We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the Profile section within the app. When you delete your account, all your personal data — including meal history, preferences, and profile information — is permanently removed from our servers.

You can also contact us at omeris343212@gmail.com to request data deletion.

Children's Privacy

Makrofy is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
